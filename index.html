Welcome to Mr. Muffler Preston

Total Car Care Centre

Mr Muffler Preston has been servicing the Northern suburbs and greater area for over 25 years assisting our customers with their automotive needs. From mechanical repairs to log book servicing, high performance exhaust systems to roadworthy certificates, our expert mechanics and friendly staff are here to help.


We are one of the largest stockers of muffler parts, exhausts and accessories in Victoria, featuring brands such as Magnaflow, Redback, Remus, Supercat and XForce. We are serious about mufflers and doubly so about fitting the right part for your car. The components we stock are those that we ourselves install on our cars and that are happy to recommend.


We also offer while you wait as well as same day drop off and pick up services, prompt service and friendly advice. You can rest assured your car is well looked after by Mr Muffler Preston.
